Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

About My Role Model

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was one of the most respected scientists and leaders in India. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contribution to India's missile and nuclear programs.

Born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he came from a humble background but achieved great success through hard work, dedication, and determination. He inspired millions of students to dream big and work towards their goals.

His famous quote:
"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."

Why He Is My Role Model

  • Great scientist and innovator
  • Simple and humble personality
  • Inspired millions of students
  • Dedicated his life to the nation
